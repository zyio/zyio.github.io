Not really got any content for here at the moment, but seeing as you're here, have some information.

This whole server is a mishmash of technologies at the moment.

This blog comes in through varnish, to an nginx backend with php-fpm and just standard MySQL.

Others on here go to an apache/passenger backend, with Sinatra running some ruby junk for me and others are just straight up LAMP.

It's all likely to change of course, it's very much a dev box with this as a bit of a public scratchpad for me.
